Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

🔒 VidDropper does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is our priority.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

VidDropper is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Video URLs you paste into the tool
  • Downloaded files — these go directly to your device
  • IP addresses or location data
  • Browser fingerprints or device identifiers

2. How the Tool Works

When you paste a URL and click Download, your request is sent to our server, which fetches the video information directly from the source platform. The file is streamed directly to your browser. No copy is retained on our servers at any point.

3. Cookies

VidDropper uses only essential functional cookies (e.g. to remember your language preference). We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. If we add Google AdSense ads in the future, Google may use its own cookies as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

VidDropper fetches video data from third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, etc.). Your use of those platforms is governed by their own privacy policies. We are not affiliated with any of these platforms.

5. Children's Privacy

VidDropper is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@viddropper.com
